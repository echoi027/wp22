Kentaro Sakaguchi
坂口健太郎

A japanese actor and fashion model


He debuted as a model in Men's Non-no magazine in 2010, and as an actor under Tristone Entertainment in 2014.
He has since starred in television series Tokyo Tarareba Musume (2017) and Signal (2018), as well as films The 100th Love with You (2017) and Color Me True (2018).
